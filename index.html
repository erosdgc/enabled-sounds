Our Label

We focus on finding a style that is mostly recognized as progressive house, but we also cover a wide variety of artists more linked to techno vibes. We want to accompany them in their careers as live performers, and that they find this is space not only to their liking, but also as place where they feel comfortable with the team and feel that they can exchange knowledge in order to grow. We love the idea that you enjoy every note and beat of music, which is what unites us.
